The Patron Housing charges €10 per person and night, regardless of room type

Room types▾
The Patron Housing has 4 room types:
  • Single rooms for individuals
  • Double rooms for marriend couples
  • Family rooms for parents with children
  • Shared rooms for people of the same gender
Amenities▾

The Patron Housing offers its users a common kitchen and dinning area and two laundry rooms

Who qualifies to stay in the Patron Housing▾

All worthy members of the Church residing in the Temple District whose primary objective is to serve in the House of the Lord
