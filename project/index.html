Welcome to the Tenerife Bikers’ Club – the ultimate community for motorcycle enthusiasts on the island. Whether you’re a seasoned rider or just starting your journey, we share the same passion for open roads, roaring engines, and the breathtaking landscapes of Tenerife. From scenic coastal cruises to thrilling mountain twisties, our rides are as much about friendship and adventure as they are about the machines we love. Join us for organized group rides, social meetups, and unforgettable events, and become part of a brotherhood and sisterhood that rides with pride, respect, and a love for freedom.

September 2025 Events

Sat 6 Sep – Teide Sunrise Ride
Meet at 5:00 AM for an unforgettable dawn ascent to Mount Teide. We’ll watch the sun break over the volcanic landscape, followed by a hearty breakfast at a mountain lodge.
Sat 13 Sep – Island Loop Challenge
A full-day 320 km circuit around Tenerife. From rugged northern coasts to winding southern passes, we’ll ride every corner of the island—fuel stops, photo ops, and ocean views included.
Sat 20 Sep – Masca Madness Ride
Conquer the legendary twisties of the Masca Valley. Expect sharp bends, cliffside views, and plenty of adrenaline. Post-ride tapas in Santiago del Teide.
Sat 27 Sep – Sunset Coastal Cruise
Gentle evening ride along the south coast, finishing at a beachside restaurant for seafood and live music under the stars.
