Site Plan

This site provides a hub for bikers and motorbikes fans in the island of Tenerife. It provides a community to share information about different models, routes etc. Thus the name: Tenerife Bikers' Club

Scenarios

  • What benefits do I get for joining the club?
  • Where can I find more information?
  • What is the best bike model for mountainous areas?
